## 🔍 Overview

<a href="#powerDirector365">PowerDirector 365</a> is CyberLink's comprehensive video editing suite for Mac — combining a full-featured non-linear timeline editor with an extensive AI-powered toolset and a continuously updated library of premium content, templates, and effects available through the 365 subscription model.

The <a href="#powerDirector365">AI-powered editing tools</a> cover a wide spectrum of production tasks — AI Background Removal isolates subjects from video footage without green screens, AI Sky Replacement swaps the sky in outdoor footage automatically, AI Motion Tracking follows moving subjects and attaches text or graphics to their path, AI Video Stabilization smooths shaky handheld footage, and AI Noise Reduction cleans up audio captured in imperfect conditions. These tools address the most time-consuming manual operations in video production, making effects accessible that would otherwise require specialized compositing skills. The <a href="#powerDirector365">multicam editing capability</a> synchronizes footage from multiple camera angles in a dedicated multicam view — switching between camera angles in real time during playback to cut a multicam sequence, a workflow essential for interviews, concerts, events, and any production using multiple simultaneous camera positions.

The <a href="#powerDirector365">motion tracking system</a> analyzes subject movement through a clip and generates a track that attached graphics, text, blurs, and effects follow automatically — applying face blurs that follow subjects through the frame, attaching product labels to moving objects, and creating graphic overlays that move with the action. The <a href="#powerDirector365">green screen and chroma key</a> removes solid-color backgrounds from video footage for compositing subjects against replacement backgrounds — the standard visual effects technique for placing subjects in virtual environments.

<a href="#powerDirector365">Color grading tools</a> include color wheels, curves, LUT support, and scopes for both basic color correction and creative color grading — bringing cinematic color treatment to Mac video production without requiring a separate color grading application. <a href="#powerDirector365">Audio ducking</a> automatically reduces background music volume when dialogue is detected — maintaining speech intelligibility over music tracks without manual volume keyframing for every music-over-dialogue transition.

<a href="#powerDirector365">4K and 8K editing support</a> handles high-resolution footage from modern cameras with hardware-accelerated playback and export using the Mac's GPU — enabling professional-resolution editing without requiring a workstation-class computer. <a href="#powerDirector365">Animated titles and effects library</a> provides a large collection of motion graphics templates, transition effects, and animated overlays that apply to the timeline without After Effects or motion graphics knowledge.
